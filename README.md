For IT Leadership:

Strategic Planning: Data-driven decisions for IT investments
Performance Management: Monitor overall IT service health
Budget Justification: Demonstrate IT value to business stakeholders
Risk Management: Identify and mitigate operational risks

For IT Operations Managers:
Team Performance: Monitor and optimize team efficiency
Incident Prevention: Identify root causes and prevent recurrences
Resource Allocation: Right-size teams and technology

Vendor Management: Track supplier performance and costs
For Finance & Business Stakeholders:
Cost Transparency: Understand IT spending and value
Budget Compliance: Ensure IT stays within financial targets

Investment ROI: Evaluate technology investment returns
Chargeback Accuracy: Fair allocation of IT costs to business units
